Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of home decor items do you offer?

We offer a wide range of home decor items including furniture, wall art, lighting, rugs, and accessories to enhance your living space.

2. How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'Order History' section. We also send tracking numbers via email.

3. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location.

4. Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, we accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in new and unused condition.

5. How do I care for my home decor items?

Care instructions vary depending on the material. Please refer to the product care guide included with your item for specific details.

6. Do you offer customization for furniture?

Yes, we offer customization for certain furniture pieces. You can choose from a range of fabrics, colors, and finishes. Please contact our customer support for more details.

7. What is your return policy for sale items?

Sale items are eligible for return, but they must be returned within 14 days of purchase, and they should be in unused condition with original tags.

8. Do you have a physical store?

Currently, we only operate online and do not have a physical storefront. However, we offer easy returns and free shipping on most items.

9. How do I know if an item will fit in my space?

We provide detailed product dimensions and sometimes even 3D models or room planners to help you visualize the item in your space. Be sure to measure your area before purchasing.

10. Can I change my order after it has been placed?

Once an order is placed, it cannot be changed. However, if you need to cancel or modify an order, please contact us within 24 hours, and we'll do our best to assist you.
