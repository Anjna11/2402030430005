Return Policy and Refunds

Return Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your home decor items, you can return most items within 30 days of receiving your order for a full refund or exchange.

Items must be in new, unused condition, and returned with the original packaging and tags.

Non-Returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable, including:

  • Customized or personalized items

  • Final sale or clearance items

  • Gift cards

  • Damaged items not reported within 7 days of delivery

How to Return an Item

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at we@heavenvibe.com within 30 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number, the items you'd like to return, and the reason for the return.
  3. Once your return request is approved, we will send you a return shipping label.
  4. Pack the items securely, include all original packaging, and affix the return label to the box.
  5. Send the package back to the provided address. You will receive a confirmation email once your return is processed.

Refund Processing

Once we receive your returned item, we will process your refund. The refund will be issued to the original payment method, and you will receive an email confirming the refund.

Refunds may take up to 10 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your bank or credit card provider.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item, please follow the return process above and specify the new item you'd like. We will process your exchange as quickly as possible, subject to availability.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. We will gladly replace the item or issue a full refund based on your preference.

Shipping Costs for Returns

If you are returning an item due to a defect or error on our part, we will cover the return shipping costs. For all other returns, customers are responsible for the return shipping fees.
