Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders within the United States. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days, and delivery can take between 3-7 business days depending on your location.

International Shipping

We ship internationally to many countries. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location. Delivery times may vary depending on the destination, but typically range from 7-14 business days.

Customs and import duties are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in the shipping charges.

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days, Monday through Friday. Orders placed over the weekend or on holidays will be processed the next business day. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number.

Shipping Restrictions

We are currently unable to ship to P.O. Boxes, APO, or FPO addresses. Additionally, we cannot ship certain large furniture items internationally. Please check product details for shipping limitations before purchasing.

Shipping Costs

Shipping is free for all domestic orders. For international orders, shipping costs are calculated at checkout and depend on your location and the size of your order.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on the carrier's website. If you have any issues with tracking, feel free to contact our customer service team.

Damaged or Lost Items

If your order arrives damaged or if you believe it has been lost in transit, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your shipment. We will assist you with filing a claim and ensuring that your order is either replaced or refunded.
